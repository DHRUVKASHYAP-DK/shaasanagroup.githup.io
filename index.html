About Us

Shaasan Seva Group is a community-driven NGO focused on building a disciplined, united, and compassionate society. Inspired by Jain values, we work for humanity, village development, youth connection, and compassion for all living beings.

Our Motto

Self Discipline Youth Connectivity Humanity

Core Activities

  • Jivdaya: Compassion towards all living beings
  • Sangh Seva: Service to society and community
  • Manav Seva: Service to humanity

Membership

Membership is open to anyone who is 18 years or above and believes in service, unity, and discipline.
